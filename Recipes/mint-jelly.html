Mint Jelly

A refreshing dessert. The steps to making this are quite simple indeed,

and when it slides down your throat, the faint, fresh flavor of mint will leave you feeling reinvigorated for the rest of the day.

Ingredients:

  • 1 Concentrated Slime From:
    • Cryo Slime
    • Anemo Slime
  • 1 Sugar
  • 1 Mint

Steps:

  1. Find a cooking station
  2. Break the Mint into powder with a mortar and pestle
  3. Mix all materials in a bowl
  4. Go to Dragonspine
  5. Store the mixture in a safe place for 3-5 hours
