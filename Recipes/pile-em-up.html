Pile 'Em Up

A rich, meaty dish. Originally a Mondstadt dish made of steaks, potatoes, and cheese.

It has since become synonymous with Ludi Harpastum.

Ingredients:

  • 1 Cheese Slice
  • 3 Potatoes
  • 3 Raw Meat
  • 1 Small Lamp Grass

Steps:

  1. Find a cooking station
  2. Boil the Potatoes
  3. Mash the Potatoes with the raw meat
  4. Make 3 Burger Patties using the mixture
  5. Fry the Burger and season with Small Lamp Grass
  6. Stack the burgers on top of each other and add the cheese slice
  7. Give the burger to a vegan for maximum emotional damage
