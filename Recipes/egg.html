Bennet's Charred Egg

Teyvat Charred Egg is a special food item that the player has a chance to obtain by cooking Teyvat Fried Egg with Bennett.

The recipe for Teyvat Fried Egg is available from the start of the game.

Ingredients:

  • 1 Bird Egg
  • Bennett

Steps:

  1. Find a cooking station
  2. Cook with an unlucky bastard (i.e bennett)
  3. Get hit by lightning
  4. Serve to unknowing customer
